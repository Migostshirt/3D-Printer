3D Printer

Info

The First 3D Printer was made in 1986 by Charles Hull. 3D Printers are made to extrude plastic and other materials. 3D Printers can print anyting you want.

Features

3D Printers take the material extrude into its organized form and after it extrudes it hardens and form the objects. The object is formed in layers until it forms the whole object. 3D printers come in all shapes and sizes and different uses.
